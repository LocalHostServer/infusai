Digital transformation

Are you ready? Reimagine how you bring together people, data, and processes to create value and stay competitive.

Engage with customers

Design personalized, data-driven customer-centric experiences to build relationships and your business.

Empower employees

Develop digital-savvy team skills and build simplified, flexible, and self-service work environments.

Transform products

Learn how to use data to help you develop and capitalize on your products and intellectual property.

Optimize operations

Identify where and how you can automate to be more adaptive, agile, and efficient.

Foster a culture of creativity and teamwork

  • Streamline process and improve productivity

Increase productivity and collaboration among content developers, editors, illustrators, and sales and service representatives by supporting efficient workflows, automated processes, and more complete information with deeper insight.

  • Drive efficiencies across your teams

Use intelligent production and post-production processes to streamline world-class media creation and delivery. Enable mobile teams and geographically dispersed teams to react quickly to insights—more securely and on most devices—so they can deliver the content your customers want right now, when they want it.

Get your content to market faster

  • Connect resources across a more flexible media supply chain

Expedite playout of radio and video content, increase productivity, and simultaneously optimise content delivery of multiple streams in multiple media formats, to any device.

  • Automate content processing and distribution models

Automate the ingestion, processing, and global distribution of your content. Index your content using AI to enable your customers to easily find it, and to enable you to recommend and monetise it.

Get the insight you need to engage and increase audience reach and frequency

  • Understand your audience segments and profiles

Better understand media interactions, consumption patterns, social sentiments, and preferences through data-driven insights across all your channels.

  • Drive revenue growth

Improve media engagement and reduce churn by applying AI to consumer experiences to personalise content and deliver relevant advertising. Help your media sales teams better manage advertising inventory while providing brands and agencies the right audience insights to optimise return on ad spend (ROAS).

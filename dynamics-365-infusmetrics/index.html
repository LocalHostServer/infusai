Configure Across Locations with Geo-fencing


Organizations can configure this app across locations enabling software to trigger a response when a mobile device enters or leaves the area within the Geo-location radius pre-defined by the organization.


Employees can easily check-in and out of their shift and also access their attendance list. Organizations can have complete visibility across locations at a glance, streamlining timesheets.

Attendance solution for Employees working from home


The employees working from home can also add their home location with the help of quick configuration, and check-In/ check out while working from home

Mobile App-Based Management System
